The Mark4 “Four Swells” framework

Every program — Wave Rider or Explorer — moves through four structured segments: Discovery, Branding, Outreach, Ignite. Each ends with a concrete outcome that flows into your Wavevault.

Think: Discovery Map → Branding Blueprint → Outreach Ramp → Ignite Vault / CRM. You don’t just “feel better”; you leave with assets, language, and a repeatable system.
Swell 1

Discovery

High-5 strengths, Priority Pie Chart, and the Discovery Map that captures your stories, wins, and non-negotiables.

Outcome: a clear Discovery Launchpad that becomes the base layer of your future narrative.
Swell 2

Branding

We translate your Discovery into a Branding Blueprint: resume language, LinkedIn, elevator stories, and proof bullets.

Outcome: your personal Branding Portfolio — what Sparkwavv calls the Branding Treasure Chest.
Swell 3

Outreach

We design your Outreach Launch Ramp — targeted contacts, warm intros, and outreach scripts calibrated for your level.

Outcome: a living Outreach Dashboard with weekly KPIs and momentum tracking.
Swell 4

Ignite

We turn your offers, conversations, and wins into an Ignite Vault / CRM so you never lose track of relationships or ramps.

Outcome: a personal Wavevault — your long-term career asset library, not just a folder of PDFs.

Questions people ask before we start

A few quick answers — we’ll cover the rest on your intro call.

How do payments and Zoom scheduling work? + −
All payments are processed securely via Stripe checkout links on this page. Once you enroll, you’ll receive a confirmation email and a Calendly link to schedule your sessions; Zoom links are auto-generated for each call.
What’s the difference between Wave Rider and Explorer? + −
Wave Rider is optimized for Exploration & Establishment stages (roughly 21–35): first job, first promotion, early pivots. Explorer is for mid-career, capstone, and encore stages (typically 35–65+), with more emphasis on positioning, legacy, and high-stakes transitions.
Can my company sponsor this as professional development? + −
Yes. Many Explorer and Wave Rider M4 clients use professional development or transition budgets. We can provide an invoice with program description, outcomes, and dates to submit to HR or your manager.
Do you work with people outside the U.S.? + −
Absolutely. Sessions are on Zoom and we regularly work across time zones. We’ll confirm logistics and regional nuances (e.g., CV vs resume, local hiring norms) during your intro call.
